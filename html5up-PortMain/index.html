How All This Works

I take a thoughtful approach, with lots of conversation, to provide you with the site you've always dreamed of.

  • Conversation
    Where it all starts. We'll cover your wants, goals, and must haves for your site.
  • Proposal
    I prepare a detailed proposal so you know exactly what I provide, when I'll provide it, and what it will cost.
  • Construction
    Once your proposal is agreed upon, you will provide me with any images and assets I may need, and I'll start building.
  • Consistant Updates
    You will receive regular updates regarding my progress to ensure the site is to your liking as we build it.
  • Launch Day
    When final payment is received, I will turn over any assets and deliverables to you and your site goes LIVE!
